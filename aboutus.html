About Us

       Sweet & Savory Bakery uses the finest ingredient and resources to bake your bread. Recipes passed down from generations, perfected over many years. Our bakery consistently produces delicious batches of bread and many other baked goods. Every item is made from the best organic ingredients as well as using no artifical flavors or colors and preservatives. We strive to set our bread at the most affordable price while being able to make it at the best quality. Our goal is to bake the best pastry for people to enjoy, it is our pleasure to make bake goods. Sweet & Savory devotes all of its effort and time into making the best and new items. We love to hear people's responses online, it gives up motivation, ideas, inspirations, and happiness to hear such great feedback! As we are only an online shop right now, we will thrive and continue to grow, producing new items while keeping up the 5 stars quality for each of our orders. We hope to see you at our bakery soon!
